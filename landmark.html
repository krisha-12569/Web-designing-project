Iconic Monuments

India is a home to some of the most awe-inspiring landmarks in the world.These Monuments reflects its rich cultural heritage and historical significance.

  • Taj Mahal: Located in Agra,this symbol of external love is one of the Seven Wonders of World
  • Red Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage site in Delhi,showing Mughal Architecture
  • Hawa Mahal: known as the "Place of Winds",this Jaipur marvel is famous for its intrecate latticework.
  • Charminar: An iconic mosque in Hyderabad and a masterpiece of Indo-Islamic architecture.
