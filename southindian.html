South Indian Cuisine

South india offers a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flavors:


Masala Dosa

A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spicy potato filling.

Idli and Sambhar

Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetable stew.

Chettinad Chicken

A fiery,flavorful chicken dish from Tamil Nadu.
